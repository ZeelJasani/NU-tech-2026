## Overview

Cyclones are powerful rotating storm systems that form over warm oceans. They bring **strong winds, heavy rainfall, and storm surges**, primarily affecting coastal regions.

---

## Causes & Warning Signs

<Image
  src="/images/Cyclones/Cyclones-2.avif"
  alt="Cyclone formation over ocean"
  width={1200}
  height={600}
/>

### Causes
Warm sea surface temperatures
Low atmospheric pressure
Favorable wind conditions

### Warning Signs
Increasing wind speed
Falling air pressure
Official cyclone warnings

---

## Preparedness

### Before a Cyclone
Secure loose objects
Strengthen doors and windows
Prepare emergency supplies

### During a Cyclone
Stay indoors in a safe room
Avoid windows  
Follow official updates  

### After a Cyclone
Avoid fallen power lines  
Check for structural damage  
Assist others if safe
