## Overview

A heatwave is a prolonged period of extremely hot weather that can seriously affect health, agriculture, and infrastructure. Heatwaves are becoming more frequent due to **climate change and urbanization**.

### Causes
- High-pressure weather systems  
- Climate change  
- Urban heat island effect  

### Warning Signs
- Extremely high temperatures  
- Heat alerts  
- Increased heat-related illnesses  

---

## Preparedness

### Before a Heatwave
- Monitor weather forecasts  
- Identify cooling centers  
- Prepare hydration supplies  

### During a Heatwave
- Stay hydrated  
- Avoid outdoor activities  
- Wear light clothing  

### After a Heatwave
- Monitor health  
- Check on vulnerable individuals  
- Resume activities gradually
